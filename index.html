About

Nomad Massage offers expert massage therapy, by a qualified therapist, in the comfort of your own home.

My name is Marine and I comes from France. With over 10 years experience behind me, I like to use a variety of techniques. I have a very intuitive approach to massage and enjoy when you let me “follow the flow”, finding the muscles that need the most focus and attention.

My ever growing knowledge on holistic well being is what I cherishes most, and likes to share.

Services

I offer a range of massage therapies, for chronic pain and to support your wellness, such as :

relaxation massage

A light to medium pressure massage, for relaxing the body and mind.

deep tissue massage

A firm pressure massage, including trigger points, to focus on specific tension and knots.

sports massage

A tonic massage to give quick relief to sore muscle and prevent injuries.

pregnancy massage

A gentle massage tailored to the needs of pregnancy aches and pain.

remedial massage

A massage to release chronic muscular tension in the deep layers of muscles, for conditions such as headaches, neck stiffness shoulder pain, back pain, leg cramps…

FAQ

How do I make a booking?

You can either send a text or an email with your preferred day and time, as well as the length of massage needed, and I’ll get back to you as soon as possible.

How do I make payment?

Payment can either be made through bank transfer or cash.

What are your areas of operation?

I operate in the South West of Christchurch, such as Halswell, Wigram, Prebbleton, Lincoln, Tai Tapu and Rolleston. For other suburbs please enquire.

What are your hours of operation?

I am at this stage only available on Saturdays and Sundays, from 9am to 5pm. Please do enquire if you can not make weekends and I’ll try to fit you in during week days.

What is your cancellation and rescheduling policy?

I request a 24 hours notice for any change or cancellation, or full payment will be required.

How long will it take to set up?

It will require 10 minutes to set up, and pack at the end of your massage. Such time will not be counted toward your massage.

What should I wear?

I apply massage oil directly onto the skin, therefore will request you to undress for your treatment. However keeping your underwear on is appreciated.

Do I need to provide anything for the therapist?

A free space in a warm room of your house will be necessary for the set up of my massage table. I can not offer massage on your own bed, as this would cause me back pain. A glass of water is also greatly appreciated!

What are your policies regarding cleanliness?

I take hygiene and sanitation very seriously. All towels will be cleaned prior to use. Before and after each treatment, hands and forearms will be washed with soap and a disinfecting solution. I expect that my customers maintain their personal hygiene as well.

Do your therapists provide any “special” services?

I do not offer any erotic or nude massage. Any behaviour deemed disrespectful will terminate your treatment and full payment will be expected.

Do you offer gift vouchers?

I do not offer physical vouchers at this stage. However you’re welcome to book and pay for a friend :)
